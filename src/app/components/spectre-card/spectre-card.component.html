Trap: Orders

Spectre draws, acts, or impersonates one of the choices. Based on the level, the boss sometimes gets to eliminate some of the options.

  • Level 1: the BOSS eliminates 0 options
  • Level 2: the BOSS eliminates 1 option
  • Level 3: the BOSS eliminates 2 options

Trap: Think Together

Two players from the squad must each write down five words relating to a the given topic. They must match a certain number of words.

  • Level 1: must match 1 word
  • Level 2: must match 2 words
  • Level 3: must match 3 words

Trap: Anagrams

One Spectre writes down as many words as possible using the given letters in thirty seconds. The words must be at least 3 letters long.

  • Level 1: write 5 words
  • Level 2: write 7 words
  • Level 3: write 9 words

Encounter: Hunter (Segue)

The active player selects the word or phrase from the two options. The Spectre and Boss take turns saying words or phrases related to the previous word or phrase.

Encounter: Generator (Showdown)

The Spectre and Boss both create lists for a topic. Whoever writes the most words in 30 seconds wins. The Boss chooses the topic.
